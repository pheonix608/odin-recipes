Burger

Introduction

A burger, also known as a hamburger, is a sandwich typically consisting of a cooked ground meat patty, most often beef, placed within a split bread roll or bun. It's a popular food item found in fast food chains, snack bars, and restaurants worldwide. Burgers can be customized with various toppings like cheese, lettuce, tomato, onion, and sauces, creating a wide range of flavors and textures.
